A little about me

I am a software engineering student at the Univeristy of Waterloo. Although I still have lots to learn, I enjoy web development and dabble a bit in design. Currently intrigued with AI, I am seeking to expand my knowledge in machine learning and the automated world.

I enjoy simple things. Whether it's how I dress or how I set up my workspace, I find having just what I need and nothing more is much more effective. Although I would not call myself a minimalist, I find that a I developped a similar mentality: I try to tackle problems be finding the simplest and most effective solution without adding too many bells and whistles. I believe that getting the job done properly outweights being flashy.

My passion for technology came from my fascination of smartphones. This fascination led me into learning about various new gadgets and keep up with the latest trends. Ultimately, I wanted to take part of such new creations which has led me into studying Software Engineering.
